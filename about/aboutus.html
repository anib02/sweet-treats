    • Our Story!

    Ani Barsamyan started Sweet Treats By Ani in July 2020. Amid the pandemic, she started her business by combining the two things she was passionate about, entrepreneurship and baking!

    She started baking around 6 years old with her grandmother and mother. Spending all her childhood summers with her grandmother, she would constantly be in the kitchen watching and eventually learning her recipes and techniques. As she grew older, she found a passion for everything and anything involving sweets.

    She strives for her business to become known worldwide for her amazing designs and taste!
